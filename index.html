<char-counter>

A simple, configurable Polymer component for adding a character counter to your form fields.

About

Adding a character counter to your form fields can be kind of a pain. Now with the convenience of web components, all you need to do is include a single <char-counter /> field. Check out the examples below to see what sort of customizations the component offers, then check out the documentation for further info.
